ABOUT THE CREATIVE TECH CONFLUENCE

Organized by BLOCK71 Saigon, supported by BCA Marketing Agency, The Creative Tech Confluence aims to create a platform for Tech Founders and Creative Personnel to meet, interact and learn from each other.

BLOCK71 is an initiative by NUS Enterprise in collaborative and strategic partnerships with established corporates and government agencies. It is a technology-focused ecosystem builder and global connector which catalyses and aggregates the start-up community. In Saigon, we spearhead new initiatives and provide mentorship and growth opportunities in key local, regional and global markets.

BLOCK71 Saigon is the collaboration between NUS Enterprise and Becamex IDC.
